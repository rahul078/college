MCA Course

CMRIT's MCA programming focuses on the areas of Application Software Development, Computer Networks, Web Design & Delopment, Database Administration, Data Mining & Warehousing, Mobile Technologies, Electronics Logic and more.The programme has been carefully Designed with focus on deliviring the latest industry oriented education in computer applications with a sound theoretical and praticalapproach to learning

Vision

To evolve as a centre of excellence in computing education producing globally competent professionals who can contribute for the betterment of society.

Eligibility Criteria

Admission to MCA Program shall be open to candidates who have passed recognized bachelor's degree of minimum of 3 years duration or equivalent examination and obtained an aggregate minimum of 50% marks taken together in all subjects including languages in all the three years of the degree examination and 45% of marks in case of sc,st and category-1 of karnataka candidates.
